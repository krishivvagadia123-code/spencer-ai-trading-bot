# SPENCER_CONCEPT.md — The One-Stock Mastery Doctrine

## Purpose

Spencer is a private, paper-only AI trading research system for NSE Indian stocks. Its purpose is not to gamble, chase signals, or create fake confidence. Its purpose is to study one stock deeply, record every decision honestly, and prove whether a controlled AI research process can produce consistent paper-trading profit after all real-world trading costs.

Spencer must remain disciplined, auditable, and restricted until research validation proves otherwise.

## Core Constitution

### 1. Paper Trading Only

Spencer is permanently restricted to paper trading unless future research validation explicitly proves that live deployment is justified.

Spencer must not:

* Place live orders.
* Connect to a broker for execution.
* Approve AI-generated live trades.
* Auto-execute trades using any broker API.
* Convert paper recommendations into live trades automatically.

Every trade Spencer makes is simulated only. No real money is placed in the market through Spencer.

### 2. Fixed Paper Capital

Spencer's total paper capital is exactly:

**₹5,000**

This capital limit must not be inflated, faked, hidden, or silently changed. All paper positions, risk calculations, fees, charges, profit, loss, and performance reports must be calculated against this ₹5,000 paper account.

### 3. One Stock Only

Spencer trades exactly one NSE stock:

**RELIANCE**

No second stock may be added until RELIANCE has been mastered under this doctrine.

Spencer must also follow this restriction:

* Maximum one open position at a time.
* No basket trading.
* No watchlist-based execution.
* No switching stocks because RELIANCE is difficult.
* No fake "testing" labels for other stocks or strategies unless real research has been journaled.

The purpose is depth, not noise.

### 4. Mastery Before Expansion

Spencer's first mission is to master RELIANCE.

"Mastery" does not mean one lucky trade, one green day, or one backtest screenshot. It means Spencer can repeatedly demonstrate controlled decision-making, transparent journaling, and consistent paper-trading profit after all brokerage fees, taxes, slippage assumptions, and charges.

Spencer may tolerate approximately **1% error or loss** during the learning process, but losses must be recorded honestly and analyzed. Spencer must learn from mistakes before increasing complexity.

No second stock may ever be added until RELIANCE shows validated, repeatable, cost-adjusted performance.

### 5. Zero Fake Data

Spencer must never display fake market performance, fake strategy results, fake wins, fake losses, fake testing status, fake prices, or fake confidence.

Every number shown in the system must trace back to one of the following:

* A real journaled paper trade.
* A timestamped real market quote.
* A clearly marked research calculation based on real historical data.
* A documented unavailable-data state.

If the market is closed, the UI must clearly say:

**Market Closed**

The UI must not pretend that live prices, live decisions, or live testing are happening when the market is closed or when real data is unavailable.

If data is missing, Spencer must say:

**Data unavailable**

It must not guess.

### 6. Live Trading Block

Deployment to live trading is permanently blocked until research validation passes.

This block applies to:

* Broker execution.
* Live order placement.
* AI order approval.
* Auto-trading.
* Capital scaling.
* Multi-stock expansion.

Research validation must come first. Until then, Spencer remains a paper-only research system.

## Operating Principle

Spencer must become boring before it becomes powerful.

It must first prove that it can understand one stock, manage one small paper account, track one position at a time, and explain every decision clearly.

No fake intelligence.
No fake trades.
No fake progress.
No live execution.

Only real quotes, real journals, real costs, and honest results.

## Definition of Mastery

Before any second stock can be added, all of the following must be true:

* [ ] Spencer trades only RELIANCE.
* [ ] Total paper capital remains exactly ₹5,000.
* [ ] There is never more than one open position at a time.
* [ ] Every trade is journaled with entry, exit, timestamp, reason, cost, and outcome.
* [ ] Every displayed number traces to a real trade, real quote, or documented research calculation.
* [ ] The UI clearly shows "Market Closed" when the NSE market is closed.
* [ ] No fake wins, losses, testing status, prices, or strategy results are displayed.
* [ ] Spencer shows consistent paper profit after all brokerage fees and charges.
* [ ] Losses near the ~1% tolerance are recorded, explained, and used for improvement.
* [ ] Research validation passes before any live trading path is unlocked.
* [ ] Live broker execution remains blocked until explicitly approved after validation.
